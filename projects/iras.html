Auto Attendance System


The Auto Attendance System is a comprehensive solution designed specifically for the College of Business and Administration. This system revolutionizes the traditional attendance process, making it significantly easier and more efficient for students.

One of the standout features of the system is its automated time in and out functionality. By utilizing the student ID numbers, students can effortlessly clock in and out in a event. This eliminates the need for manual attendance taking, saving valuable time for both students and Council Officers. With a simple input of their ID numbers, the system automatically records the exact time of their entry and exit, ensuring accurate attendance records.

To further enhance accuracy and reliability, the system integrates a real-time clock. This feature ensures that the attendance records are always up to date and synchronized with the current time. By capturing the precise time at which students clock in and out, the system provides administrators with an accurate representation of attendance data.

The system also offers extensive data filtering capabilities. This means that attendance records can be easily sorted and filtered based on various criteria. Administrators can extract specific information from the attendance data, such as filtering attendance for a particular time range, date, event, or even year level. This flexibility allows for in-depth analysis of attendance patterns, facilitating better insights and decision-making. and this filtering system can be also use in verifying attendance for clearance.

The system benefits students by streamlining their attendance process and ensuring accurate records. It also empowers administrators with valuable insights and reporting capabilities, enabling them to effectively manage attendance and make data-driven decisions.
